Why B.Tech?

What is B.Tech?

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a 4-year undergraduate program that focuses on applying scientific and engineering principles to solve real-world problems.

Program Structure

The 4-year program is divided into 8 semesters with a mix of theoretical subjects, practical lab sessions, projects, and internships.

Practical Focus

B.Tech emphasizes hands-on learning through laboratory work, workshops, and industry-oriented projects.

Global Recognition

The degree is recognized worldwide, opening opportunities for higher studies and careers internationally.

Eligibility Criteria

To pursue a B.Tech program in India, candidates must meet specific academic requirements and clear entrance examinations.

Academic Requirements

  • Completed 10+2 from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM)
  • Minimum 50-75% aggregate in PCM (varies by college)
  • For Biotechnology, Biology may be accepted instead of Mathematics

Entrance Exams

  • JEE Main - For NITs, IIITs, and other top colleges
  • JEE Advanced - For admission to IITs
  • State Exams - MHT CET, KCET, AP EAMCET, etc.
  • University Exams - BITSAT, VITEEE, SRMJEEE
